Sandwiches

All of our Sandwiches are served with French Fries or Sweet Potato Fries and a Pickle.
Our Breads and Rolls are made fresh in-house. Gluten-Free options are available.

Lobster Roll

Classic fresh Maine Lobster Salad, served on a fresh baked buttered roll.

New Orleans Pulled Pork

66 spice rubbed prime pork shoulder, smoked in-house over maplewood and roasted for hours, served on a fresh baked bun with horseradish coleslaw and cheddar

Fish Tacos

Talapia filet in a marinade of tequila, jalapeno, and lime juice, baked and topped with a fresh pineapple slaw, crema mexicana, and guacamole, served in warm corn tortillas

THE Burger

Char-Grilled Certified Angus Beef ® served on a fresh house-made bun.
add cheese: american, cheddar, monterey jack, pepper jack, swiss or bleu cheese.
add grilled mushrooms, onions, peppers or jalapeños.
add Bacon, Beef Chili, Guacamole or a Fried Egg.
or add The Works!

Vegetarian option: substitute our house-made veggie burger served with lettuce, tomato and red onion.

Haddock Sandwich

(grilled, baked, fried or blackened) fresh north atlantic haddock filet on a seasoned and grilled, house-made hoagie roll with lettuce, red onion and tomato.
served with your choice of remoulade, tartar or cocktail sauces.

Cali Veg

avocado, tomato, cucumber, sprouts, red onion, vermont cheddar cheese, lettuce and a dill aioli served on grilled wheat bread

Club

66-style club with turkey, lettuce, tomato, mayonnaise, and applewood smoked bacon, served on a choice of grilled bread (white, wheat, or rye)

Po'Boy

fried shrimp, clams, oysters, or silver dollar mushrooms in a fresh baked roll, chopped romaine, coleslaw, tomato red onions, pickles, remoulade and crushed red pepper

Reuben

nyc deli style reuben stacked high with corned beef served on fresh-made rye bread w/ sauerkraut, swiss cheese and 1000 island dressing

Steak Bomb

Tuna Steak Sandwich
