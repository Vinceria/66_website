Our Restaurant


66 Steak & Seafood is located in the historic coastal town of Blue Hill in a building with a rich and diverse history. Serving Downeast Maine since June of 2011, 66 has quickly become a regular spot for many local residents as well as a yearly tradition for returning summer vacationers.
