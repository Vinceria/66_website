Entrees

All of our entrees are prepared fresh and only to your order and served with your choice of Side.

Whole Maine Lobster

You can have your lobster Steamed, Boiled, Baked or Stuffed.
Stuffed is a customer favorite, with our special shrimp and scallops stuffing and then baked to perfection!

Steak Tips

Char-grilled Certified Angus Beef® Pub Steak in a south pacific marinade.

Surf & Turf Platter

Certified Angus Beef® Pub Steak, Shrimp, Scallops and fried Whole Belly Clams.
Shrimp and Scallops can be served Grilled, Fried or Blackened.

St. Louis BBQ Ribs - All You Can Eat

66 spice rubbed prime pork ribs are maplewood smoked in-house,
then roasted for hours in Coca-Cola and brushed with our house made 66 BBQ sauce.

BBQ Trio

Sliced Certified Angus Beef® Pub Steak, Grilled Chicken breast and slow roasted St. Louis Style Ribs.
All brushed with our house made 66 BBQ sauce.

Fish N' Chips

Fresh caught North Atlantic Haddock, lightly battered then fried and served with french fries.
You may be exchange fries for another Side of your choice. Haddock can also be prepared Grilled, Baked or Blackened.

Seafood Platter

Order your platter Grilled, Baked, Fried or Blackened and with either Calamari or Gulf Shrimp or Maine Whole Belly Clams or Maine Scallops.
Also served with your choice Remoulade, Tartar or Cocktail Sauce.

Fried Seafood Combo

A fantastic mix of Maine Whole Belly Clams, Shrimp, Scallops, Haddock and Calamari.
Served with your choice Remoulade, Tartar or Cocktail Sauce.

Seafood Paella

Spanish Seafood Stew with Shrimp, Scallops, Mussels, Grilled Chicken and house-ground Chorizo Sausage in a Lobster stock.
Served with Saffron Rice, Tomatoes, cracked Red Pepper and Scallions.

Jambalaya

Traditional cajun stew with Shrimp, Scallops, Andouille Sausage, Chicken, Smoked Ham, Rice, Peppers and Onions.
Served in Large or Small portions and to your choice of Mild, Medium or Hot.
